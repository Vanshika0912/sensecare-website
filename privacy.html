Privacy Policy

At Sense Care, your privacy is our priority. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information while ensuring transparency and trust in every interaction.

1. Introduction

Sense Care is dedicated to providing child development and neurotherapy services in a compassionate, safe, and respectful environment. In doing so, we handle certain personal details shared by parents, guardians, or clients. This policy outlines how we safeguard that information.

By using our website, booking appointments, or visiting our center, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to offer you the best therapeutic and consultation experience.

2.1 Information You Provide

When you contact or register with us, we may collect the following details:

  • Parent/guardian and child’s name
  • Contact number and email address
  • Medical or developmental history relevant to therapy
  • Appointment and payment details (if applicable)

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you browse our website, certain technical data may be automatically collected to enhance your experience:

  • Device and browser information
  • Anonymous website usage statistics
  • Cookies to remember preferences (you may disable these anytime)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information responsibly to provide a smooth and personalized experience.

  • To schedule and manage appointments
  • To communicate therapy progress or updates
  • To respond to inquiries and provide support
  • To maintain records for ongoing therapeutic care
  • To improve our services and ensure quality standards

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

Your personal information is never sold or shared for commercial purposes. We share it only under specific circumstances:

4.1 With Your Consent

We may share relevant information with therapists, caregivers, or professionals directly involved in your child’s care, only with your permission.

4.2 Legal and Safety Requirements

We may disclose limited information if required to do so by law or to protect the safety and well-being of our clients and staff.

5. Data Security

We take your privacy seriously and follow strict security measures to protect your information:

  • All online communications are protected with SSL encryption
  • Client records are securely stored and accessible only to authorized staff
  • We regularly review and update our security protocols

However, please note that no system is completely immune to risks, and we encourage clients to use secure channels when sharing sensitive information.

6. Your Rights and Choices

As a valued client, you have the right to:

  • Access and review your personal or your child’s information
  • Request correction of inaccurate details
  • Withdraw consent for information sharing at any time
  • Request deletion of your personal records (subject to legal requirements)

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the details below.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect service improvements or legal updates. When we make changes, we’ll revise the “Effective Date” above and post the updated version on this page.

We encourage you to review this page regularly to stay informed about how we protect your information.
